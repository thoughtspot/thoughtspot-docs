View the insights into your data, through the ThoughtSpot Liveboard.

Explore the Liveboard that your administrator recommended to you in ThoughtSpot's xref:business-user-onboarding.adoc[business user onboarding].

Notice who created this Liveboard, and who uses it.

You can xref:liveboard-follow.adoc[follow] this Liveboard, so you get notifications for major changes to the data it represents.

With Liveboards, you can xref:search-drill-down.adoc[drill down] to a chart or table's underlying data for a deeper understanding, or follow an AI-guided exploration of a visualization or headline metric using xref:answer-explorer.adoc[Answer Explorer].
You can filter on the Liveboard's data, or on an individual visualization's data.
