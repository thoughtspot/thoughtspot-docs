[#matillion-etl]
== Matillion ETL job
To deploy the {spotapp}, you must load your ServiceNow data into your cloud data warehouse. ThoughtSpot partners with https://www.matillion.com/[Matillion^] to make the ETL process easier with a custom Matillion job. This job loads your ServiceNow data into your *Snowflake* environment. If you use a different cloud data warehouse, you cannot use this job.

You can access the job https://exchange.matillion.com/s/exchange-job/a074G00001LQjBiQAL/service-now-incremental-load-for-thoughtspot-spotapp-on-snowflake[here^].
