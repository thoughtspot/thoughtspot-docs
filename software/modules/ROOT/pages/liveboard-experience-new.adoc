Learn about ThoughtSpot's new Liveboard experience, and how to enable it.

== Overview

The new Liveboard experience contains multiple new features and feature enhancements.
It is part of ThoughtSpot's move to a new stack.
It is *_off_* by default for individual users, but the option to turn it on or off <<toggle-on-off,individually>> is *_on_* by default. Administrators can manage the availability of the new Liveboard experience for the whole cluster from the <<global-enablement,Admin Console>>.

The new Liveboard experience is dependent on the xref:answer-experience-new.adoc[new answer experience]. To use the new Liveboard experience, you must first xref:answer-experience-new.adoc#toggle-on-off[enable the new answer experience]. The new Answer experience is on by default.

If the new Liveboard experience is available in your environment, turn it on or off individually from your profile, in the *Experience manager* at the bottom of your profile.

[#toggle-on-off]
== Turn the new Liveboard experience on or off individually

The new Liveboard experience is *_off_* by default. You can turn it on or off individually from your profile.

NOTE: The new Liveboard experience is dependent on the new answer experience; you must enable the new answer experience before you enable the new Liveboard experience.

To turn the new Liveboard experience on or off, follow these steps:

. Navigate to your profile: select the *Profile* icon at the upper right of your screen, and select *Profile*.
. Next to *Experience*, at the bottom of your profile, select *Edit*.
. Ensure that the new answer experience is on. Next to *Answers experience*, select *New experience*.
. Next to *Liveboards experience*, select *New experience* or *Classic experience*.
. Select *Save*.

[#global-enablement]
== Turn the new Liveboard experience on or off for the whole cluster

You can manage the availability of the new Liveboard experience from the Admin Console.
You can turn the new Liveboard experience on or off globally, and specify the default Liveboard experience for all users.
For example, you can turn the new Liveboard experience on globally, but still have the classic experience as the default, allowing users to opt into the new experience.

To manage the new Liveboard experience, follow these steps:

. Ensure that the xref:answer-experience-new.adoc[new answer experience] is on for the whole cluster.

. Select *Admin*, from the top navigation bar.

. Select *Search & SpotIQ*.

. Scroll down to *ThoughtSpot Experience*, and select *Edit*.

. Enable or disable the new Liveboard experience.

. If you enable the new Liveboard experience, you can specify whether it should be on by default for all users, or if they should have to opt in.
+
To display the new Liveboard experience to all users, enable the *new Liveboard experience* toggle, then enable the *Make new Liveboard experience default* toggle.
+
To display the classic Liveboard experience to all users, but allow them to turn the new experience on for themselves in their profile, enable the *new Liveboard experience* toggle, then disable the *Make new Liveboard experience default* toggle.
