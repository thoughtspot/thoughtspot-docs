== Reset a Liveboard visualization

After performing ad-hoc actions or edits to a visualization of your Liveboard, you can reset the visualization to its original form.

To reset a visualization:

. On an altered visualization, click the reset icon that appears at the lower right of the visualization.
+
image::reset_a_visualization-old.png[]

. Save your Liveboard: select the *More* menu icon image:icon-more-10px.png[more options menu icon] and selecting *Save*.
